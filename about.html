Experience

The experience behind the method.

I've spent 11 years in Dallas real estate, but what sets me apart isn't the years, it's what I did with them. I didn't just sell homes for other people. I bought a house and DIY-flipped it while living in it (not recommended for the faint of heart, highly recommended for your education). I bought a duplex, house-hacked it, and still own it as a rental today. I found a wholesale deal, flipped it with a GC, and sold it.

And right now, I'm living in Lake Highlands in a home I chose strategically, for the schools, the neighborhood trajectory, and yes, the move-up potential I've already mapped out.

Origin

Why the Move Up Method exists.

The thing I kept seeing, over and over, with client after client, was that smart, capable people had no idea they were already positioned to make a move. They didn't know what equity was. They didn't know they were sitting on it. And they definitely didn't know they could use it to leapfrog into a completely different financial position without starting over from scratch.

So I built the Move Up Method. Education before the transaction.
